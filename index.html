About

I'm a member of Planet Lab, where I contribute to the development of a systems model for planetary health. My work focuses on collecting, cleaning, and preparing data to support the team's analysis of planetary spheres within the model. With research interests in economics and planetary health, I'm especially motivated by understanding how human development interacts with Earth systems to shape both human and ecological well-being.

I'm currently taking a gap year after graduating from high school, and I bring a fresh perspective to the team while building my skills in data management and systems-oriented analysis. Outside of my work, I enjoy cooking Asian cuisine and playing the flute.
